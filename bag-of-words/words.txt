The International Conference on Machine Learning (ICML) is the
leading international academic conference in machine learning. Along
with NeurIPS and ICLR, it is one of the three primary conferences of
high impact in machine learning and artificial intelligence research.[1] It
is supported by the (IMLS). Precise dates vary year to year, but paper
submissions are generally due at the end of January, and the
conference is generally held the following July. The first ICML was held
1980 in Pittsburgh.
